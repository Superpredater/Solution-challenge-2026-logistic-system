# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Smart Supply Chain Optimization is a real-time logistics intelligence system that monitors millions of concurrent shipments across global transportation networks. The system proactively detects transit disruptions — including weather events, carrier delays, port congestion, geopolitical instability, and active conflict zones — before they compromise delivery timelines. It provides dynamic rerouting recommendations, predictive risk scoring, AI-based ETA prediction, autonomous decision-making, digital twin simulation, carbon optimization, and automated stakeholder notifications to minimize supply chain disruption impact. The platform also exposes a rich API ecosystem and supports edge computing for offline environments.

## Glossary

- **System**: The Smart Supply Chain Optimization platform as a whole
- **Shipment**: A tracked unit of cargo moving through the supply chain with an origin, destination, and expected delivery timeline
- **Disruption**: Any event that causes or is predicted to cause a deviation from a shipment's planned route or delivery timeline
- **Risk_Score**: A numeric value between 0 and 100 representing the unified probability and severity of a disruption affecting a Shipment, incorporating weather, operational, war-state, and geopolitical factors
- **Route**: An ordered sequence of transit nodes (ports, warehouses, carriers) connecting a shipment's origin to its destination
- **Reroute_Recommendation**: An alternative Route generated by the system to avoid or mitigate a Disruption
- **Transit_Node**: A physical or logical point in the supply chain network (port, warehouse, distribution center, carrier hub)
- **ETA**: Estimated Time of Arrival — the predicted delivery timestamp for a Shipment
- **Alert**: A notification sent to stakeholders when a Disruption is detected or a Risk_Score threshold is exceeded
- **Stakeholder**: A user or system with a vested interest in a Shipment's status (shipper, consignee, logistics manager)
- **Data_Ingestion_Pipeline**: The subsystem responsible for collecting and normalizing data from external sources (weather APIs, carrier feeds, port authority systems, news feeds)
- **Risk_Engine**: The subsystem responsible for computing unified Risk_Scores and detecting Disruptions
- **Routing_Engine**: The subsystem responsible for generating and evaluating Reroute_Recommendations
- **Notification_Service**: The subsystem responsible for delivering Alerts to Stakeholders
- **Dashboard**: The user-facing interface for monitoring shipment status, disruptions, recommendations, and global risk
- **Map_Visualizer**: The interactive map component of the Dashboard displaying shipment locations, routes, disruptions, and risk overlays
- **AI_Predictor**: The subsystem responsible for machine-learning-based ETA prediction and demand forecasting
- **Decision_Engine**: The subsystem responsible for autonomous rerouting decisions and decision logging
- **Digital_Twin**: The simulation subsystem that models the supply chain network for scenario testing
- **Carbon_Calculator**: The subsystem responsible for computing CO₂ emissions per Shipment and evaluating eco-friendly routing options
- **Carrier_Risk_Profiler**: The subsystem responsible for scoring individual carriers based on historical performance and integrating scores into the Risk_Score
- **Anomaly_Detector**: The subsystem responsible for detecting route deviations, fraud, and theft patterns
- **Edge_Agent**: A lightweight agent deployed at remote Transit_Nodes for offline tracking and local data buffering
- **Geopolitical_Risk_Index**: A numeric value between 0 and 100 representing the level of geopolitical risk for a geographic region, derived from news intelligence and political event analysis
- **War_State**: A classification of a geographic region's conflict status — one of: Safe, Caution, High_Risk, or Restricted
- **Collaboration_Workspace**: The in-platform communication and task management interface scoped to individual Shipments

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Real-Time Shipment Tracking

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want to monitor all active shipments in real time, so that I have full visibility into the current state of the supply chain.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support tracking of at least 1,000,000 concurrent active Shipments.
2. WHEN a Shipment's location or status is updated by a carrier or Transit_Node, THE System SHALL reflect the updated state within 30 seconds.
3. WHEN a Shipment's ETA changes by more than 60 minutes, THE System SHALL recalculate the Risk_Score for that Shipment.
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display the current location, Route, ETA, and Risk_Score for each active Shipment.
5. IF a Shipment update cannot be processed due to a data format error, THEN THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL log the error with the raw payload and continue processing subsequent updates.

---

### Requirement 2: Disruption Detection

**User Story:** As a supply chain analyst, I want the system to automatically detect transit disruptions before they impact delivery timelines, so that I can take corrective action proactively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN external data sources report a weather event, carrier delay, port closure, or infrastructure incident, THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL ingest and normalize the event within 60 seconds of publication.
2. WHEN a new event is ingested, THE Risk_Engine SHALL evaluate all Shipments whose Routes intersect the affected Transit_Nodes or geographic regions.
3. WHEN the Risk_Engine determines that a Disruption affects a Shipment, THE Risk_Engine SHALL update the Shipment's Risk_Score within 2 minutes of the triggering event being ingested.
4. THE Risk_Engine SHALL detect operational bottlenecks by identifying Transit_Nodes where average dwell time exceeds the historical 90th-percentile dwell time for that node.
5. IF the Risk_Engine cannot reach an external data source for more than 5 minutes, THEN THE System SHALL flag all Shipments transiting through that data source's coverage area with a Risk_Score of at least 50 and generate an Alert for affected Stakeholders.

---

### Requirement 3: Predictive Risk Scoring

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want each shipment to carry a continuously updated risk score, so that I can prioritize my attention on the highest-risk shipments.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Risk_Engine SHALL assign a Risk_Score between 0 and 100 to every active Shipment, where 0 represents no detected risk and 100 represents near-certain disruption.
2. WHEN a Shipment's Risk_Score is recalculated, THE Risk_Engine SHALL incorporate current weather conditions, carrier on-time performance history, Transit_Node congestion levels, remaining transit time, and the Geopolitical_Risk_Index of regions along the Route as input factors.
3. WHEN a Shipment's Risk_Score increases by 20 or more points within a 1-hour window, THE Risk_Engine SHALL classify the change as a Risk_Escalation and trigger an Alert.
4. THE Risk_Engine SHALL recalculate Risk_Scores for all active Shipments at least once every 15 minutes, independent of event-driven recalculations.
5. WHERE a Stakeholder has configured custom Risk_Score thresholds, THE Notification_Service SHALL send an Alert when a Shipment's Risk_Score crosses the configured threshold.

---

### Requirement 4: Dynamic Rerouting Recommendations

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want the system to suggest alternative routes when disruptions are detected, so that I can minimize delays, costs, and safety risks.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Shipment's Risk_Score exceeds 70, THE Routing_Engine SHALL generate at least one Reroute_Recommendation for that Shipment.
2. WHEN generating a Reroute_Recommendation, THE Routing_Engine SHALL evaluate alternative Routes that optimize for the lowest combined estimated cost, transit time, and safety risk, and SHALL exclude Routes that pass through regions classified as War_State Restricted.
3. WHEN a Reroute_Recommendation is generated, THE Routing_Engine SHALL include the estimated new ETA, estimated cost delta, and the specific Disruption being avoided.
4. THE Routing_Engine SHALL generate Reroute_Recommendations within 5 minutes of a qualifying Risk_Score threshold being reached.
5. IF no viable alternative Route exists for a disrupted Shipment, THEN THE Routing_Engine SHALL notify the assigned Stakeholders with the reason no alternative is available and the current estimated delay.
6. WHEN a Stakeholder accepts a Reroute_Recommendation, THE System SHALL update the Shipment's active Route and recalculate the ETA accordingly.

---

### Requirement 5: Stakeholder Alerting and Notifications

**User Story:** As a shipper or consignee, I want to receive timely alerts about disruptions affecting my shipments, so that I can coordinate with downstream partners and manage expectations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Disruption is detected for a Shipment, THE Notification_Service SHALL deliver an Alert to all Stakeholders associated with that Shipment within 5 minutes of the Disruption being confirmed.
2. THE Notification_Service SHALL support delivery of Alerts via email, SMS, and webhook.
3. WHEN an Alert is delivered, THE Notification_Service SHALL record the delivery timestamp, channel, and recipient for audit purposes.
4. IF an Alert delivery attempt fails, THEN THE Notification_Service SHALL retry delivery up to 3 times with exponential backoff before marking the Alert as undeliverable and logging the failure.
5. WHERE a Stakeholder has configured a quiet period, THE Notification_Service SHALL suppress non-critical Alerts (Risk_Score below 70) during that period and deliver a digest at the end of the quiet period.
6. THE Notification_Service SHALL deduplicate Alerts such that a Stakeholder does not receive more than one Alert for the same Disruption on the same Shipment within a 30-minute window.

---

### Requirement 6: Data Ingestion and External Integration

**User Story:** As a platform operator, I want the system to ingest data from diverse external sources reliably, so that risk assessments are based on current, accurate information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL support ingestion from at least the following source types: REST APIs (polling), webhook push feeds, and message queue subscriptions (Kafka, SQS).
2. WHEN data is received from an external source, THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL normalize it into the canonical internal event schema before forwarding it to the Risk_Engine.
3. THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL validate all incoming data against the canonical schema and reject records that fail validation with a structured error log entry.
4. WHEN a previously unavailable external data source becomes reachable again, THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL resume ingestion and process any backlogged events in chronological order.
5. THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL maintain an ingestion throughput of at least 50,000 events per minute under normal operating conditions.

---

### Requirement 7: Historical Analytics and Reporting

**User Story:** As a supply chain analyst, I want access to historical disruption and performance data, so that I can identify systemic weaknesses and improve future planning.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL retain Shipment tracking data, Risk_Score history, Disruption records, and Alert logs for a minimum of 24 months.
2. WHEN a Stakeholder requests a report for a specified date range and filter criteria, THE System SHALL return the report within 30 seconds for queries spanning up to 90 days of data.
3. THE Dashboard SHALL provide pre-built report templates for: on-time delivery rate by carrier, disruption frequency by Transit_Node, and average Risk_Score trend over time.
4. THE System SHALL export report data in CSV and JSON formats.

---

### Requirement 8: Access Control and Multi-Tenancy

**User Story:** As a platform operator, I want each customer organization to see only their own shipment data and have appropriately scoped permissions, so that data isolation, security, and governance are maintained.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL enforce tenant-level data isolation such that a Stakeholder in one tenant organization cannot access Shipment, Disruption, or Alert data belonging to another tenant.
2. WHEN a Stakeholder attempts to access a resource outside their tenant scope, THE System SHALL return an authorization error and log the attempt.
3. THE System SHALL support role-based access control with at least the following roles: Viewer (read-only Dashboard access), Analyst (read + report generation), Manager (read + report + reroute approval), and Admin (full configuration, user management, and tenant administration).
4. WHEN a Stakeholder's session token expires, THE System SHALL require re-authentication before granting further access.
5. THE System SHALL authenticate all Stakeholder sessions using JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with a configurable expiry period.
6. WHERE a tenant has enabled multi-factor authentication, THE System SHALL require a Stakeholder to complete a second authentication factor before granting access.
7. THE System SHALL enforce role-based restrictions at both the API and Dashboard UI layers, such that UI controls and API endpoints unavailable to a Stakeholder's role are not accessible.

---

### Requirement 9: Real-Time Map Visualization

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want an interactive map showing live shipment positions, risk levels, and disruption overlays, so that I can assess the global supply chain state at a glance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL display all active Shipments on an interactive world map with color-coded markers reflecting each Shipment's current Risk_Score (green for 0–39, amber for 40–69, red for 70–100).
2. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL render active Route paths and Disruption event overlays on the map.
3. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL display congestion heatmaps and risk heatmaps derived from aggregated Risk_Score data across geographic regions.
4. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL render War_State overlays that visually distinguish regions classified as Safe, Caution, High_Risk, and Restricted, with Restricted regions displayed in red.
5. WHEN underlying Shipment or Disruption data changes, THE Map_Visualizer SHALL update the map display within 30 seconds.
6. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL provide filter controls allowing a Stakeholder to restrict the map view by region, carrier, and Risk_Score range.
7. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL support a historical playback mode that replays Shipment positions and Risk_Score states over a user-selected time range.

---

### Requirement 10: AI-Based ETA Prediction

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want AI-generated ETA predictions for each shipment, so that I can provide accurate delivery commitments to customers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI_Predictor SHALL generate an ETA prediction for every active Shipment using a machine learning model trained on historical transit data, carrier performance, and route characteristics.
2. THE AI_Predictor SHALL achieve a mean absolute percentage error of less than 10% on ETA predictions measured against actual delivery timestamps on the held-out validation dataset.
3. THE AI_Predictor SHALL retrain the prediction model at least once every 24 hours using the most recent available historical data.
4. WHEN a Shipment's actual delivery timestamp is recorded, THE AI_Predictor SHALL incorporate the outcome as a feedback signal for subsequent model training cycles.
5. WHEN the AI_Predictor generates an ETA prediction, THE System SHALL display the predicted ETA alongside a confidence interval on the Dashboard.

---

### Requirement 11: Autonomous Decision Engine

**User Story:** As a platform operator, I want the system to autonomously execute rerouting decisions for high-risk shipments, so that response time is minimized without requiring manual intervention for every event.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Shipment's Risk_Score exceeds 70 and the Decision_Engine has been enabled for autonomous operation by an Admin, THE Decision_Engine SHALL automatically apply the highest-ranked Reroute_Recommendation without requiring manual Stakeholder approval.
2. THE Decision_Engine SHALL log every autonomous decision with the triggering Risk_Score, the selected Reroute_Recommendation, the timestamp, and the identity of the system actor.
3. WHEN a Stakeholder with the Manager or Admin role issues a manual override for an autonomous decision, THE Decision_Engine SHALL revert the Shipment to the manually specified Route within 2 minutes and log the override.
4. THE System SHALL provide a Decision_Engine audit trail accessible to Admin and Manager roles showing all autonomous decisions and overrides for a configurable lookback period.

---

### Requirement 12: Digital Twin Simulation

**User Story:** As a supply chain analyst, I want to simulate disruption scenarios against a digital twin of the supply chain, so that I can evaluate the impact of hypothetical events before they occur.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Digital_Twin SHALL model the current supply chain network including all active Shipments, Routes, Transit_Nodes, and carrier capacities.
2. THE Digital_Twin SHALL support simulation of at least 10,000 concurrent Shipments within a single scenario run.
3. WHEN a Stakeholder defines a disruption scenario (including war-zone activation, port closure, or carrier delay), THE Digital_Twin SHALL compute the projected impact on affected Shipments' ETAs and Risk_Scores and present the results within 60 seconds.
4. THE Digital_Twin SHALL support scenario parameters including: geographic conflict zone activation, Transit_Node closure, carrier capacity reduction, and weather event injection.
5. WHEN a simulation completes, THE Digital_Twin SHALL generate a summary report including the number of affected Shipments, average ETA deviation, and recommended mitigation actions.

---

### Requirement 13: Carbon Optimization

**User Story:** As a sustainability officer, I want the system to calculate and minimize the carbon footprint of shipment routes, so that the organization can meet its environmental commitments.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Carbon_Calculator SHALL compute the estimated CO₂ emissions in kilograms for every active Shipment based on the transport mode, distance, and carrier emissions factors.
2. WHEN generating a Reroute_Recommendation, THE Routing_Engine SHALL include the estimated CO₂ delta between the current Route and the alternative Route.
3. WHERE a Stakeholder has enabled eco-friendly routing preference, THE Routing_Engine SHALL rank Reroute_Recommendations that minimize CO₂ emissions above recommendations that minimize cost or time, provided the CO₂-optimized route does not increase transit time by more than 20%.
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display the cumulative CO₂ emissions for all Shipments within a tenant over a user-selected time period.

---

### Requirement 14: Carrier Risk Profiling

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want the system to maintain risk profiles for each carrier, so that carrier reliability is factored into routing and risk decisions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Carrier_Risk_Profiler SHALL maintain a risk profile for each carrier, incorporating on-time delivery rate, incident history, and capacity reliability over a rolling 90-day window.
2. WHEN the Risk_Engine calculates a Shipment's Risk_Score, THE Risk_Engine SHALL incorporate the Carrier_Risk_Profiler score for the carrier assigned to each leg of the Shipment's Route.
3. WHEN a carrier's on-time delivery rate drops below 80% over the most recent 30-day period, THE Carrier_Risk_Profiler SHALL flag the carrier as High_Risk and notify Admin Stakeholders.
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display carrier risk profiles and trend charts accessible to Analyst, Manager, and Admin roles.

---

### Requirement 15: Demand Forecast Integration

**User Story:** As a supply chain planner, I want the system to predict demand zones and prioritize shipments accordingly, so that high-demand deliveries are protected from disruption.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI_Predictor SHALL generate demand forecasts for configurable geographic zones on a rolling 7-day horizon, updated at least once every 24 hours.
2. WHEN a demand forecast identifies a zone with predicted demand exceeding a configurable threshold, THE System SHALL increase the priority classification of Shipments destined for that zone.
3. WHEN the Routing_Engine generates Reroute_Recommendations, THE Routing_Engine SHALL apply higher priority weighting to Shipments with elevated demand-zone priority classifications.
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display the current demand forecast heatmap alongside the active Shipment map for Analyst, Manager, and Admin roles.

---

### Requirement 16: Multi-Modal Route Optimization

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want the system to optimize routes across air, sea, rail, and road transport modes, so that the most efficient combination of modes is used for each shipment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Routing_Engine SHALL support route evaluation across at least four transport modes: air, sea, rail, and road.
2. WHEN generating a Reroute_Recommendation, THE Routing_Engine SHALL evaluate hybrid multi-modal routes that combine two or more transport modes where doing so reduces cost, transit time, or Risk_Score compared to single-mode alternatives.
3. WHEN a multi-modal Reroute_Recommendation is generated, THE Routing_Engine SHALL include the mode sequence, Transit_Node handoff points, and estimated time and cost for each leg.
4. THE Routing_Engine SHALL exclude transport modes that are unavailable due to War_State Restricted classifications or active Disruptions on the relevant infrastructure.

### Requirement 17: Collaboration Workspace

**User Story:** As a logistics team member, I want a shared workspace per shipment where I can communicate and assign tasks with colleagues, so that coordination on disrupted shipments is centralized and auditable.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Collaboration_Workspace SHALL provide a per-Shipment threaded message channel accessible to all Stakeholders associated with that Shipment within the same tenant.
2. WHEN a Stakeholder posts a message in a Collaboration_Workspace channel, THE System SHALL deliver the message to all other active participants within 5 seconds.
3. THE Collaboration_Workspace SHALL support task creation, assignment to a named Stakeholder, due date setting, and status tracking (Open, In Progress, Resolved).
4. THE System SHALL retain Collaboration_Workspace messages and task history for the same retention period as Shipment tracking data (minimum 24 months).
5. WHEN a Disruption Alert is generated for a Shipment, THE System SHALL automatically post a summary of the Alert into the Shipment's Collaboration_Workspace channel.

---

### Requirement 18: Anomaly Detection

**User Story:** As a security and logistics manager, I want the system to detect unusual shipment behavior, so that route deviations, fraud, and theft can be identified and acted upon quickly.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Anomaly_Detector SHALL monitor all active Shipments for deviations from their planned Routes exceeding a configurable distance threshold (default: 50 kilometers).
2. WHEN a route deviation is detected, THE Anomaly_Detector SHALL generate an Alert classified as High_Priority and notify the assigned Stakeholders within 2 minutes of detection.
3. THE Anomaly_Detector SHALL apply behavioral pattern analysis to identify Shipments exhibiting patterns consistent with cargo theft or fraud, including unexpected stops, unauthorized Transit_Node visits, and abnormal dwell times.
4. WHEN the Anomaly_Detector identifies a potential fraud or theft pattern, THE System SHALL escalate the Alert to Admin Stakeholders and log the event with full Shipment state at the time of detection.

---

### Requirement 19: Edge Computing and Offline Tracking

**User Story:** As a platform operator, I want shipment tracking to continue functioning in areas with limited or no connectivity, so that visibility is not lost in remote transit zones.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Edge_Agent SHALL buffer Shipment location and status updates locally when network connectivity to the central System is unavailable.
2. WHEN network connectivity is restored, THE Edge_Agent SHALL synchronize all buffered updates to the central System in chronological order within 60 seconds of reconnection.
3. THE Edge_Agent SHALL continue to apply locally cached routing and risk rules during offline periods to provide best-effort local guidance.
4. WHEN an Edge_Agent has been offline for more than 30 minutes, THE System SHALL flag all Shipments associated with that Edge_Agent as connectivity-impaired and generate an Alert for Admin Stakeholders.

---

### Requirement 20: API Ecosystem

**User Story:** As a third-party developer, I want well-documented REST and GraphQL APIs with reliable rate limiting, so that I can build integrations and applications on top of the platform.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL expose all core platform capabilities (Shipment tracking, Risk_Score retrieval, Reroute_Recommendations, Alerts) through both a REST API and a GraphQL API.
2. THE System SHALL enforce per-tenant, per-API-key rate limits configurable by Admin Stakeholders, and SHALL return a structured rate-limit-exceeded error response when a limit is reached.
3. THE System SHALL provide a developer analytics dashboard accessible to Admin Stakeholders showing API call volume, error rates, and latency percentiles per API key over a rolling 30-day window.
4. WHEN a breaking change is introduced to the API, THE System SHALL maintain the previous API version for a minimum of 90 days and notify registered API consumers at least 30 days before deprecation.

### Requirement 21: War-State Monitoring

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want the system to monitor active conflict zones and automatically adjust risk scores and routing decisions, so that shipments are protected from war-related disruptions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Risk_Engine SHALL continuously monitor authoritative conflict and geopolitical data sources and classify each geographic region with a War_State of Safe, Caution, High_Risk, or Restricted.
2. WHEN a region's War_State is updated, THE Risk_Engine SHALL re-evaluate the Risk_Score of all Shipments whose Routes pass through or near that region within 5 minutes of the classification change.
3. WHEN a region is classified as High_Risk or Restricted due to active conflict, THE Risk_Engine SHALL increase the Risk_Score of all affected Shipments by a minimum of 30 points, not to exceed the maximum value of 100.
4. WHEN a region is classified as Restricted, THE Routing_Engine SHALL exclude all Routes passing through that region from Reroute_Recommendations.
5. WHEN a region's War_State changes to High_Risk or Restricted, THE Notification_Service SHALL immediately generate a Critical Alert for all Stakeholders with Shipments in the affected region, bypassing any configured quiet periods.
6. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL display War_State overlays on the interactive map, with Restricted regions rendered in red, High_Risk in orange, Caution in yellow, and Safe regions with no overlay.
7. THE Risk_Engine SHALL monitor for airspace closures and naval blockades in addition to land-based conflict zones and apply War_State classifications accordingly.

### Requirement 22: Geopolitical News Intelligence

**User Story:** As a supply chain analyst, I want the system to ingest and analyze global news for geopolitical risk signals, so that emerging political threats are detected before they escalate into physical disruptions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL ingest news articles and event feeds from at least three authoritative global news and geopolitical intelligence sources in real time, with a maximum ingestion latency of 60 seconds from publication.
2. WHEN a news article or event is ingested, THE Risk_Engine SHALL apply natural language processing to extract geopolitical event signals including sanctions announcements, political instability indicators, and trade restriction declarations.
3. THE Risk_Engine SHALL maintain a Geopolitical_Risk_Index between 0 and 100 for each monitored geographic region, updated within 5 minutes of a relevant news event being processed.
4. WHEN a region's Geopolitical_Risk_Index increases by 20 or more points within a 24-hour window, THE Risk_Engine SHALL treat the change as a Disruption and trigger re-evaluation of all Shipments transiting that region.
5. THE Dashboard SHALL display a geopolitical news feed panel showing the most recent ingested events with their extracted risk signals, region tags, and impact on the Geopolitical_Risk_Index.
6. THE Data_Ingestion_Pipeline SHALL support ingestion of news content in at least five languages, normalizing extracted signals into the canonical internal schema regardless of source language.

### Requirement 23: Unified Global Risk Engine

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want a single unified risk score per shipment that combines all risk dimensions, so that I have one authoritative signal for decision-making.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Risk_Engine SHALL compute each Shipment's Risk_Score as a weighted combination of at least four risk dimensions: weather and environmental risk, operational and carrier risk, War_State risk, and Geopolitical_Risk_Index.
2. WHEN any individual risk dimension changes, THE Risk_Engine SHALL recompute the unified Risk_Score for all affected Shipments within 2 minutes.
3. THE Routing_Engine SHALL use the unified Risk_Score as the primary input for all routing decisions, ensuring that routes with elevated war-state or geopolitical risk are deprioritized relative to lower-risk alternatives.
4. THE Map_Visualizer SHALL display a global risk heatmap derived from the aggregated unified Risk_Scores of all active Shipments across geographic regions.
5. THE Digital_Twin SHALL support simulation of conflict scenario injection into the unified Risk_Engine to project the impact of hypothetical geopolitical or war-state escalations on the active Shipment portfolio.

### Requirement 24: Global Alert Prioritization

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want alerts to be classified by severity so that critical geopolitical and war-related events receive immediate attention regardless of notification preferences.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Notification_Service SHALL classify every Alert with one of three severity levels: Informational (Risk_Score 0–39), Warning (Risk_Score 40–69), or Critical (Risk_Score 70–100).
2. WHEN an Alert is triggered by a War_State change to High_Risk or Restricted, or by a Geopolitical_Risk_Index increase of 20 or more points, THE Notification_Service SHALL classify the Alert as Critical regardless of the current unified Risk_Score.
3. WHEN a Critical Alert is generated, THE Notification_Service SHALL deliver the Alert to all affected Stakeholders immediately, bypassing any configured quiet periods or digest schedules.
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display a global risk alert panel showing all active Critical and Warning Alerts, sorted by severity and recency, accessible to all authenticated Stakeholders within the tenant.
5. THE Notification_Service SHALL include the Alert severity classification in all delivered Alert payloads across email, SMS, and webhook channels.

### Requirement 25: Gemini AI Integration

**User Story:** As a logistics manager, I want AI-powered explanations, summaries, and intelligent suggestions powered by Gemini, so that I can understand complex risk situations and make better decisions faster.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL integrate with the Gemini API to provide explainable AI capabilities for Risk_Score changes, Disruption events, and Routing decisions.
2. WHEN a Shipment's Risk_Score changes significantly, THE AI_Service SHALL use Gemini to generate a natural language explanation of the contributing factors and present it on the Dashboard.
3. THE System SHALL provide a natural language chatbot interface powered by Gemini that allows Stakeholders to query shipment status, risk summaries, and routing options using plain language.
4. WHEN geopolitical news events are ingested, THE AI_Service SHALL use Gemini to generate concise summaries of the events and their projected supply chain impact.
5. WHEN the Anomaly_Detector identifies a suspicious pattern, THE AI_Service SHALL use Gemini to generate an explanation of the detected anomaly and suggested response actions.
6. THE AI_Service SHALL generate automated narrative reports summarizing supply chain performance, disruption trends, and risk outlook using Gemini.
7. THE AI_Service SHALL respond to all Gemini-powered queries within 5 seconds under normal operating conditions.
8. IF the Gemini API is unavailable, THE System SHALL fall back to rule-based explanations and log the degraded-mode event.
9. THE System SHALL log all AI interactions including query, response, timestamp, and Stakeholder identity for audit purposes.
